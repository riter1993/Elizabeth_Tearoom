Elizabeth Tearoom

Elizabeth aims to bring the nostalgic taste of holidays in Europe at your favorite Afternoon tea spot in London or favorite little bakery in Paris to Japan.

Elizabeth Tearoom started in 2021 as people were not able to travel to Europe and were craving Western sweets. After getting numeral requests from friends who knew about my years living abroad and craved a taste of those goodies, it then escalated into a business.

I believe that the taste, smell and appearance of food can be extraordinarily evocative, bringing back memories not just of eating food itself but also of places you’ve been and people you’ve met.

Elizabeth is the result of all the experiences I’ve had while abroad, all the cities I’ve been to and all the loved ones who had taught me something about their food and shared a meal with me.
