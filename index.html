Elizabeth Tearoom aims to bring the nostalgic taste of European tea and baking to Japan.

Elizabeth Tearoom

As a fix for cravings of Western sweets during the COVID-19 lockdown and getting numerous requests from friends who longed for a taste of those goodies, the baking escalated into a business to what is now known as Elizabeth Tearoom.

We believe that the taste, smell, and appearance of food can be extraordinarily evocative by bringing back memories not just of eating food itself, but also of places you’ve been and people you’ve met. That’s why we use authentic, traditional, and passed down recipes from people we have personally met, spent time with, and lived with while abroad.

Elizabeth is the result of moments experienced while living and traveling abroad, an homage to the destinations, and to all the loved ones who have shared not only something about their food, but shared a meal with us..
